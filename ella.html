A Majestic Arrival at African Premium Zoo in 2011

About Ella

Ella, a majestic African elephant, made her grand arrival at the African Premium Zoo in 2011. Since then, she has become a symbol of grace and strength, admired by visitors for her wisdom and gentle demeanor. With her towering stature and gentle spirit, Ella continues to charm and inspire everyone who visits the zoo.

Over the years, she has been a part of various conservation efforts and educational programs, raising awareness about the importance of preserving her species. Her journey from the wild to becoming an ambassador for elephants at the zoo has been an inspiring one.
